Photography Club

Photography Club is one of the most integral and popular clubs of the BMSCE. A heaven for the creative, the Film section of the club will host events like Movie Making, Trailer Making, Ad-Making. After a hiatus of around a decade or so, the club will restart monthly movie screenings along with live screenings too. The club will also be conducting various workshops and learning sessions on image-editing and video-editing softwares. As far as photography is concerned, with events like Graphic Designing, Photography, Photo walks you would definitely take out your cameras and get clicking. The club will initiate a set of fun filled events like photo walks in Bangalore, weekly comic strips, online photography contests, treasure hunts etc. It is definitely one of the most active and fun clubs in BMSCE.
